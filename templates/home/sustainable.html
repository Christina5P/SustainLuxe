Definition of Carbon Savings

By "carbon savings," we refer to the amount of greenhouse gases, measured in carbon dioxide equivalents (COâ‚‚e) in kilograms, required to produce a similar new item. By choosing second-hand products, you can make a positive impact by reducing the emissions associated with manufacturing new items.

Assumptions Used in Our Carbon Savings Calculation

To estimate carbon savings, we have made a few assumptions based on various sources. We collect data on the materials used in the clothing we handle. Based on this, we make assumptions about the material composition of each garment to calculate an average carbon savings figure per item.

Sources Used

  • Charitable Reuse
  • Textile Exchange
